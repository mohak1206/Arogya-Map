Algorithms & Concepts

The computer science behind HealMaps

Haversine Distance

Great-circle distance between two GPS coordinates for shortest path calculations.

Priority Queue

Sorting patients by severity: Critical > High > Medium > Low for optimal assignment.

Greedy Allocation

Assigning hospitals with the maximum available resources weighted by proximity.

Deadlock Prevention

MySQL Transactions (BEGIN, COMMIT, ROLLBACK) for safe concurrent bed booking.
